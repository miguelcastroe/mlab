Insight

Before language becomes decoration

Summary
Insight is not the moment when language becomes sharper. It is the moment when understanding changes. In AI-assisted work, a sentence can sound true before it has earned that truth. This node separates observation from recognition: what happened is not yet the insight; the insight is the human meaning that changes how the observation is understood.
Risk
Fluent phrase mistaken for truth.
Practice
Recognize what was not literal.
Output
Human insight.
Applied bite
Start from the observation and look for the human truth beginning to appear. Do not look for a brilliant phrase. Look for a recognition: something that changes how the observation is understood.
